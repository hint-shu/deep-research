## Key facts

- **Free:** 1,000 credits/month (no credit card)
- **Project:** $30/мес — 4,000 credits — $0.0075/credit
- **Bootstrap:** $100/мес — 15,000 credits — $0.0067/credit
- **Startup:** $220/мес — 38,000 credits — $0.0058/credit
- **Growth:** $500/мес — 100,000 credits — $0.005/credit
- **Pay-as-you-go:** $0.008/credit — no monthly commit
- **Enterprise:** custom
- **Credits shared across all endpoints**: search, extract, map, crawl, research
- **Tavily Search basic:** 1 credit/request
- **Tavily Search advanced:** дороже (не указано в выдаче, но раскрывается в доке)

## Key quotes

> "You get 1,000 free API Credits every month. No credit card required."
> "Tavily operates on a simple, credit-based model"
> "Credits are shared across all API operations such as search, extract, map, and crawl."

## Notes

- Confirms: Tavily pricing tiers matching independent reviews
- **Разница с Firecrawl:** Firecrawl's 500 credits — one-time, Tavily's 1000/month — recurring → Tavily заметно выгоднее на free tier
- При 100K credits: Tavily Growth $500/мес vs Firecrawl Standard ~$83/мес — Firecrawl ~10× дешевле на этом объёме (но credits не эквивалентны — Firecrawl credit = 1 scrape, Tavily credit зависит от endpoint)
