## Key facts

- **Free:** 500 credits (one-time, no card), 2 concurrent, low rate limits
- **Hobby:** $16/мес — 3,000 credits/мес, 5 concurrent, basic support
- **Standard:** ~$83/мес — 100,000 credits/мес, 50 concurrent (данные из независимых ревью)
- **Growth:** ~$333/мес — 500,000 credits/мес, 100 concurrent
- Extra credits: $9 per extra 1k credits on Hobby plan
- Биллинг: annual discount — сохраняет ~$38/мес
- **Credit cost:** 1-5 credits per scrape depending on features (proxy pool, etc)
- Dedicated "Extract" plans: $89-719/мес for token-based billing (separate from scrape credits)
- Enterprise: custom pricing (∞ credits)
